Spicy Cuisine

The Style of India

Are you Hungry!
Curries:
1.Paneer Tikka Masala: Cubes of paneer cheese marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in a creamy tomato-based gravy.
2.Dal Makhani: Creamy lentils cooked with butter, cream, and aromatic spices, served with naan or rice
3.Malai Kofta: Deep-fried vegetable and paneer dumplings served in a creamy, mildly spiced gravy
4.Dal Tadka: Yellow lentils tempered with ghee, cumin, garlic, and red chilies, garnished with fresh coriander.

Accompaniments:
1.Naan: Oven-baked flatbread made with refined flour and served hot.
2.Roti: Whole wheat flatbread cooked on a griddle and served fresh.
3.Rice: Fragrant basmati rice, plain or flavored with spices like cumin or saffron.
4.Raita: Yogurt-based side dish with grated cucumber, carrots, and spices, served chilled.
5.Vegetable Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables, herbs, and spices, served with raita.
